Q: How did you come up with the idea?

A: We wanted to use technology to help people. One day a dear friend of us, her name is Eugenia, asked us how she could grow her housekeeping company market. We asked if she had a website, she replied she did not. What about social medias, we followed. She said she had an Instagram and Facebook account, but she never go there to update her pages. We checked available solutions and there where all too complex, too expensive, and too time consuming for her. And that's how the idea of building a fully automated social media marketing platform adapted to small and medium businesses was born.

Q: How much does it cost?

Q: Is it a subscription?

A: Nope. It's on-demand, $0.49 per post. It's fully adaptable to your needs. You set the posting frequency.

Q: Can I post less than once a day?
